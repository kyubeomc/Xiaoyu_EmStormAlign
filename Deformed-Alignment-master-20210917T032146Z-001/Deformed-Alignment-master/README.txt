These source codes are distributed as accompanying codes for the article "Deformed Alignment of Super-Resolution Images for Semi-flexible Structures in 3D" by Shi et al @ Bo Huang group.

The provided package includes three Matlab softwares that do model-free fast alignemnt of super-resolution images:
(1) 2D deformed alignment of super-resolution images; 
(2) 3D alignment; 
(3) 2-color 3D alignment;  

Each software is distributed with examples used in the article.
